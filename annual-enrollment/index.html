2018 Benefits Annual Enrollment Instructions

October 2 – October 18, 2017


Annual Enrollment is your once-a-year opportunity to elect the benefits that best fit your family’s needs. All benefits you elect during Annual Enrollment become effective January 1, 2018.

You have three ways to enroll in benefits for 2018:

  1. By Phone: Call the HBC Benefits Service Center at 1-800-498-8705 to enroll with a Benefits Counselor who will explain your benefits, answer your questions and record your elections.

  3. Online: Visit hbc.benefitsnow.com to access the online enrollment system. If it is your first time accessing the system, click on “Are you a new user?” and follow the prompts to register and create your own login credentials. You will need the following information to verify your identity and set up your account:
    • Last four digits of your social security number
    • Your date of birth

    Once you set up your account, sign in, select Annual Enrollment, then click “Enroll Now” to begin.
