SignUpGenius FAQs

QI am no longer able to attend the appointment I booked. How do I edit my sign up or RSVP?

If you signed up without an account (using your name and email address only)- you can get to the sign up by clicking the same link you clicked to originally RSVP. When you get to the sign up page, you'll see a link at the top in the ‘General Details’ area that says, "Already signed up? You can change your sign up."

If you signed up with an account (using an email and password)- go to SignUpGenius.com >>Log-in >> My Account page >> "Invited To" tab>> Click the pencil icon to the right of the item and you will be able to edit your RSVP or Click the “X” to delete your RSVP.

QDo I have to register as a user for your site to sign up for a scheduled event?

No, SignUpGenius does not make you register on their site to sign up for an event.

QDoes SignUpGenius sell or pass on my information in any way?

No. SignUpGenius does not sell or give out membership information to any third party vendors. They never sell or pass on user profile information. Plus, they make sure that your email is never publicly displayed on the internet where someone unauthorized could grab it.

QI can't remember my password! What do I do?

Underneath every login box there is a "Forget Your Password" link that will allow you to email yourself a link to reset your password. If you try that link and it says that you never registered then you may have mistyped your email address when you signed up. If that's the case, contact SignUpGenius’ support staff and they'll do their best to track down your login info for you. Contact support:http://www.signupgenius.com/help

If you don't receive any email response from them after using the "Forget Your Password" link, check your spam folder. If it’s still not there, contact their support team directly and they'll reset your password for you: http://www.signupgenius.com/help

QHow do I change my name, email, or password?

You can update any of your profile information at any time. Log into the site with your email and password.

To change your name or email, click "Settings" on the left side of your account page>> Make update, click "Update Profile" at the bottom of the page. Once you log out, and log back in, the edited name will show at the top of your account page.

To change your password, click the "Password" tab on the "Settings" page.
