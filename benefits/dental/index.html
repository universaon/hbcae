Dental


Maintaining good oral health is essential to maintaining good overall health. The HBC Dental Plans provide coverage for a wide range of dental services and supplies through Cigna. You may choose from one of three coverage options. Plan highlights are provided below for the Core and Buy-Up plans. For a list of costs per service under the DHMO, see the Cigna Dental benefit schedule. The Core and Buy-Up dental plan options use the Total Cigna Dental PPO network, and the DHMO uses the Cigna Dental Care network.

Alternate Benefit Provision

In situations where there are two acceptable procedures to treat a dental condition, Cigna will provide coverage for the least costly Dental Service that is appropriate.


Example: In the case of a tooth in need of a filling, there are different material options available. If the dentist chooses to perform a composite resin (tooth colored) filling on a molar tooth, Cigna will provide coverage for a less-costly, appropriate Amalgam (silver) filling. The Amalgam filling is an acceptable form of restoration that is strong in make-up and is less costly. Because the molar tooth is outside of the normal smile line, the use of composite for aesthetic reasons is not needed.


Pre-Treatment Review

To protect you from unexpected dental expenses, Cigna recommends you have your dentist submit your treatment plan to Cigna for review prior to non-emergency services when work in excess of $200 is proposed.
