Adoption Assistance


Thinking about adding to your family? HBC provides financial assistance if you adopt a child under age 16. The HBC Adoption Assistance Program reimburses up to $5,000 of covered expenses per child, for up to two children. Full-time and part-time Associates scheduled to work 20 or more hours per week with at least one year of service are eligible for Adoption benefits. Here’s how the plan works:


    Covered Expenses
  • Licensed adoption agency and placement fees (including foreign adoption)
  • Legal fees and court fees
  • Transportation and lodging costs to bring the child to the adoptive parents
  • Immigration, immunization and translation fees

    Expenses that are Not Covered
  • Expenses related to the adoption of your spouse’s child
  • Any adoption that results in a violation of federal or state law
  • A surrogate parenting arrangement
  • Medical expenses of the birth mother or child prior to the adoption
  • Temporary foster care homes
  • Fees for services not yet performed

How to Request Reimbursement

Once the adoption is final, complete an Adoption Assistance Claim Form and return it to the HBC Benefits Department (250 Highland Park Blvd. Wilkes-Barre, PA 18702; Attn: Ruth Noss). Include itemized receipts for expenses listed and proof of adoption.
