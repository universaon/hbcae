FSA Grace Period

Saks and Lord & Taylor Associates

If you are currently enrolled in a 2016 Flexible Spending Account (FSA), you have until March 15, 2017, the Grace Period, to use your funds. Your current Health Care FSA (HCFSA) will automatically transition to a Limited Purpose FSA on January 1, 2017, for the Grace Period. The Limited Purpose FSA may only be used for dental and vision expenses. Below are details on your 2016 FSA:

  • Medical expenses must be incurred (services received) by December 31, 2016 to be eligible for reimbursement.
  • Dental and vision expenses may be incurred through March 15, 2017.
  • Dependent care expenses may be incurred through March 15, 2017.
  • Claims for all 2016 expenses may be submitted through June 15, 2017, the Run-out Period.
  • Associates with a 2016 HCFSA balance who enrolled in a 2017 HCFSA will receive their new payment cards prior to 1/1/17. Your remaining 2016 balance and your 2017 HCFSA balance will be available on your card. Associates with a remaining 2016 HCFSA balance who did not enroll in a 2017 HCFSA may continue to use their 2016 card through June 15, 2017, the Run-out Period.


Gilt Associates

If you are currently enrolled in a 2016 Flexible Spending Account (FSA), you have until March 15, 2017, the Grace Period, to use your funds. Your current Health Care FSA (HCFSA) will automatically transition to a Limited Purpose FSA through ConnectYourCare (CYC) on January 1, 2017, for the Grace Period. The Limited Purpose FSA may only be used for dental and vision expenses. Below are details on your 2016 FSA:

  • Medical expenses must be incurred (services received) by December 31, 2016 to be eligible for reimbursement.
  • Dental and vision expenses may be incurred through March 15, 2017.
  • Dependent care expenses may be incurred through March 15, 2017.
  • Effective 1/1/17, Claims for all 2016 expenses may be submitted to CYC through, June 15, 2017, the Run-out Period.
  • Associates with a 2016 HCFSA balance who enrolled in a 2017 HCFSA will receive their payment cards from CYC prior to 1/1/17. Your remaining 2016 balance and your 2017 HCFSA balance will be available on your card. Associates with a remaining 2016 HCFSA balance who did not enroll in a 2017 HCFSA will receive their payment card late January.


Paper claims for 2016 expenses may be submitted to WageWorks until December 23, 2016, and electronic claims may be submitted until December 31, 2016. A black-out period will then take place to allow for the transition of claims administration to CYC beginning late January 2017.
