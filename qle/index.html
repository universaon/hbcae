Qualifying Life Event
Enrollment Instructions

Have you had a qualifying life event? Eligible life events may include:

  • Getting Married or Divorced
  • Having a Baby or Adopting a child
  • Death
  • You or your spouse’s employment status changes
  • You or your dependent’s eligibility changes
  • Your Address changes (and your current plan is no longer available)

If you have a qualifying life event, you can make related changes to your benefits as long as you complete your enrollment, including submitting appropriate documentation, no later than 31 days from the date of the event, using one of the following enrollment options:

  • Online: Visit hbc.benefitsnow.com to access the online enrollment system. If it is your first time accessing the system, click on "Are you a new user?" and follow the prompts to register and create your own login credentials. You will need the following information to verify your identity and set up your account:
    • Last four digits of your social security number
    • Your date of birth

    Once you have set up your account, sign in, select your event from the “Life Events” tab, and submit your Qualifying Life Event. Then, click “Enroll Now” to begin.

  • By Phone: Call the HBC Benefits Service Center (1-800-498-8705) to discuss your options, ask your questions and make your enrollment elections with a Benefits Counselor.

Please note, it may take 5-10 business days from your date of hire or status change for your information to be available in the enrollment system. If that is the case, you will have 31 days from the date your record is available to make your elections.


You MUST complete the enrollment process within 31 days of your date of hire or date of status change to elect or waive benefits. If you do not take action, you will not have coverage other than the HBC-provided benefits.


Dependent Verification

If you elect coverage for a spouse, domestic partner or civil union dependent, you MUST complete the Certification of Marriage, Civil Union or Domestic Partnership. The certification must be returned via fax at 1-844-397-7261 within 31 days of enrollment. Click here to access the form.


Confirmation Statement

All full-time and part-time Associates eligible for HBC health and welfare benefits should receive an email at the end of their enrollment period with instructions to access a confirmation statement online. If you do not have an email address on file, a confirmation statement will be mailed to your home address within 31 days of enrollment. This statement will list your benefit elections, if any, and the corresponding rates/contribution, including company-provided benefits. Be sure to review the confirmation statement carefully to ensure it accurately reflects your elections. If any corrections are necessary, call the HBC Benefits Service Center during their regular hours.
